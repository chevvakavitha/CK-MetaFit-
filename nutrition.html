Smart Nutrition Plans

Eating a variety of fresh green vegetables is essential for balanced nutrition. They provide vitamins, minerals, and fiber that support overall health and digestion.

  • ✅Include leafy greens like spinach and kale daily
  • ✅Wash vegetables thoroughly before eating
  • ❌Avoid overcooking to preserve nutrients
  • ❌Don’t rely only on supplements for vitamins

Smart snacking with nutrient-rich options helps maintain energy and prevents overeating. Choose snacks that nourish your body and keep you satisfied.

  • ✅Opt for nuts, seeds, or fresh fruits as snacks
  • ✅Plan and portion snacks to avoid excess
  • ❌Avoid sugary, salty processed snacks
  • ❌Don’t snack while distracted or stressed

Hydration plays a key role in maintaining bodily functions including digestion, brain health, and energy. Drinking enough water daily is critical for your wellness journey.

  • ✅Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily
  • ✅Include water-rich foods like cucumbers and watermelon
  • ❌Limit sugary beverages and sodas
  • ❌Don’t wait until thirsty to drink water
